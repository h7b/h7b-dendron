# TikZJax

- GitHub: https://github.com/artisticat1/obsidian-tikzjax
- Thoughts:
    - A plugin for Obsidian that lets you render LaTeX and TikZ diagrams in your notes
    - Currently supported packages
        - chemfig
        - tikz-cd
        - circuitikz
        - array
        - amsmath
            - amstext
        - pgfplots
        - tikz-3dplot
